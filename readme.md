AI Ayurveda Health Assistant

A web-based AI-assisted wellness platform that brings ancient Ayurvedic knowledge into a modern digital interface. The system allows users to explore natural remedies, analyze symptoms, and learn about Ayurvedic principles such as the three Doshas (Vata, Pitta, Kapha).

This project demonstrates how traditional Indian medical knowledge can be integrated with modern technology to improve accessibility and health awareness.

Project Overview

Ayurveda, meaning “Science of Life,” is a 5000+ year old holistic medical system originating in India. This project aims to digitize Ayurvedic knowledge and make it accessible through an intelligent web-based assistant.

The system helps users:

Identify possible health conditions from symptoms

Discover Ayurvedic herbs and remedies

Learn traditional health practices

Understand Ayurvedic body constitution

The application functions as a digital wellness guide based on Ayurvedic principles.

Key Features
Symptom Analyzer

Users can describe their health concerns and the system analyzes symptoms to provide Ayurvedic guidance.

Outputs may include:

Possible conditions

Natural remedies

Herbal suggestions

Lifestyle recommendations

Ayurvedic Herbs Library

A searchable database containing detailed information about Ayurvedic herbs including:

Benefits

Traditional uses

Preparation methods

Safety precautions

Natural Remedies Guide

A curated collection of more than 100 traditional Ayurvedic remedies categorized by health conditions such as:

Digestive issues

Respiratory problems

Skin conditions

Stress and mental health

Joint and muscle pain

Ayurveda Knowledge Section

Educational section explaining Ayurvedic concepts including:

The Three Doshas (Vata, Pitta, Kapha)

Ayurvedic lifestyle practices

Preventive health principles

Balance between body, mind, and environment
